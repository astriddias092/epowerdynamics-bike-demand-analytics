# ePowerDynamics: Predictive Analytics & Bike Hire Demand Optimization

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing and modeling a year's worth of micro-mobility data for **ePowerDynamics** to optimize fleet management, stabilize supply levels, and reduce resource wastage. By applying descriptive statistics, bivariate probability models, and multiple linear regression, this analysis identifies critical operational drivers and delivers data-driven strategic recommendations directly to the corporate board.

## Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Tools:** Microsoft Excel (Advanced Data Analysis Toolpak, PivotTables, Dynamic Dashboards)
* **Analytics Frameworks:** Descriptive Statistics, Confidence Intervals, Hypothesis Testing (t-Tests), Bivariate Probability Analysis, Correlation Matrices, Multiple Linear Regression Modeling.

## Key Business Insights
* **Weather & Seasonal Dominance:** Clear weather conditions dramatically maximize rental velocity (accounting for over 61% of operational days). Summer consistently yields the highest rental demand, significantly exceeding the corporate baseline target of 75 daily bike hires.
* **The Temperature Driver:** Temperature exhibits a powerful positive correlation (**0.771**) with bike demand, identifying it as the single strongest environmental metric for predicting customer behavior.
* **Product Segmentation:** Pedal bikes dominate consistent weekly weekday commuting patterns, while electric bikes (eBikes) experience sharp spikes in demand during peak leisure windows (Fridays and Saturdays).

## Strategic Board Recommendations
1. **Gamified Off-Peak Engagement:** Implement a dynamic point-based loyalty system during transitional seasons (Spring/Autumn) and low-demand periods to smooth out revenue dips.
2. **Targeted Fleet Allocation:** Shift high-maintenance eBike inventory toward high-probability weekend leisure periods to maximize asset utilization, while keeping pedal bike allocations optimized for weekday working commuter routes.
3. **External Partnerships:** Form strategic marketing alliances with eco-conscious tourism operators and hotels to capture high-margin tourist segments during peak clear-weather windows.
